Scope of Services

Six Pillars of Our Brokerage

Everything you need to be properly insured — delivered by specialists at zero cost to you.

01
Risk Assessment, Analysis & Management
  • Identifying, evaluating and analyzing client's business operational and personal risks
  • Assessing current insurance gaps and exposure to financial loss
  • Preparing risk profiles and insurance requirement assessments
  • Professional guidance on risk prevention and mitigation strategies
  • Offering training or awareness sessions on insurance and risk management for client's staff
02
Advisory & Consultation
  • Advising clients on suitable insurance products and coverage options
  • Providing independent, unbiased comparisons among different insurers
  • Recommending optimal insurance programs that balance cost, coverage, and risk protection
  • Ongoing review and analysis of insurance policies including coverage adequacy and claims analysis
03
Policy Placement & Administration
  • Negotiating with insurers to obtain competitive terms, conditions, and premiums
  • Guiding and assisting in preparing and submitting insurance proposal forms
  • Reviewing and confirming policy wording to ensure relevancy and adequacy of coverage
  • Monitoring policy expiration dates and coordinating timely renewals
04
Financial Consideration
  • Competitive premium quotations through market bidding across 22+ insurers
  • Structuring insurance premiums in line with client's financial goals
  • Guidance on avoiding over-insurance or under-insurance
  • No service fees to clients — our commission is paid by the selected insurer
05
Claims Management & Support
  • Guiding and assisting clients in preparing, submitting, and pursuing insurance claims
  • Liaising with insurers to ensure fair and prompt claim settlement
  • Dedicated claims support team — expediting claim service
  • Ongoing follow-up until final settlement of all claims
  • Intermediating in claims negotiations and resolving disputes
06
Continuous Client Support
  • Offering continuous advisory support throughout the policy period
  • Providing updates on new insurance products, legal, and regulatory changes
  • Maintaining transparent and ethical relationships with clients and insurers
  • Providing timely insights on insurance market conditions and emerging risks

How do I get started with ABIB? ›

No. We charge absolutely no service fees to clients. Our commission is paid by the selected insurance company.
