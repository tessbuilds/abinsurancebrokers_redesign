Who We Are

A Trusted Partner in Ethiopia's Insurance Landscape

Abiy and Binyam Insurance Brokers is a trusted partner in Ethiopia's insurance landscape. For more than three decades, we have provided expert brokerage services, offering clear guidance, dependable support, and tailored insurance solutions to individuals, families, and businesses across the country.

Our team brings deep industry expertise, strong insurer relationships, and a proven track record serving clients in sectors ranging from construction and transportation to hospitality, real estate, manufacturing, and agriculture.

We understand that every client faces unique risks. That is why we take a consultative approach — beginning with careful listening. By learning your goals, challenges, and operations, we design insurance solutions that respond to real needs and offer lasting security.
