Insurance Bonds
Financial guarantee bonds for business contracts and government tenders
5 Products
Advance Payment Bond
▼

Guarantees that advance payments made by a client to a contractor will be repaid if the contractor fails to fulfil their contractual obligations. Required for most government and large commercial contracts in Ethiopia.

Get a Quote →
Performance Bond
▼

Guarantees that a contractor will complete a project according to the terms of the contract. If they fail, the bond compensates the client for losses incurred. Mandatory for most Ethiopian government tenders.

Get a Quote →
Bid Bond
▼

Guarantees that a bidder will enter into a contract and provide the required performance bond if awarded a tender. Protects the employer from bidders who withdraw after winning. Required for most public tenders in Ethiopia.

Get a Quote →
Maintenance Bond
▼

Covers the defects liability period after project completion. Guarantees the contractor will rectify any defects that arise during the maintenance period specified in the contract.

Get a Quote →
Customs Bond
▼

A financial guarantee required by Ethiopian Customs Authority to secure payment of import duties and taxes on goods temporarily imported or stored in bonded warehouses. Essential for importers and freight forwarders.
